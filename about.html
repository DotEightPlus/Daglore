About Us

At Daglore Model Schools, we are committed to laying solid foundation in children in all aspect from kindergaten, and buiding them through to our secondary school.

The school has demonstrated outstanding leadership in the education of children since inception and is a reference point in education delivery, discipline and morals.

Our activities; academic, social and other co-curricular, are intended to nurture, identify and develop potential abilities of childeren with a view to making them contribute meaningfully and taking lead.

Our strength lies with our staff who continue to make sacrifices to ensure that every child life is transformed.
